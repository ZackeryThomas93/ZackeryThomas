What is Vector-21

Vector-21 is a 3rd-person survival action game. Bob wakes up in a mysterious pyramid, desperate to live, he escapes to find himself on an island. Your companion Echo and you must discover the trials of the islands ahead, unraveling the mysteries left behind from humanity, and survive.

Development

Crazy Carrot Studios is a indie developer creating their first game Vector-21.

My specific roll in this project is leaf programmer. Leading a team using scrum to complete programming tasks, and create fun game mechanics and game loops. This is consists of coding UI, combat, survival, crafting systems, and world building with cinematics. This is the largest project I have worked on, and we are looking to release a demo and trailer this fall.
