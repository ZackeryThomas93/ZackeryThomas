Development

My name is Zackery Thomas, I have grown up in the golden age of games and technology advancement. Natrually this has led me into a passion for game production and design. I started at Boise State University in 2019 and entered in to gaming, interactive media, and mobile development program (GIMM). Here I have learned many new skills, XR development in Swift, Unity, and Unreal. HTML, CSS, Javascript, C#, C++, Blueprint are some of the many languages I have learned for mobile and web development.

My goal is to find a project I am passionate about and a team to join in order to build something to leave an impact in people's lives. I believe the best things I can bring to the table is my work ethic, drive for accomplishment, coming in every day being the best I can be, and development of strong relations with others to overcome the many challenges teams can face, whether in programming or life.
